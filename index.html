Developed a website for a local bakery business, allowing customers to browse options available for sizes, flavors and types of baked goods. Also allows them to place orders and customize their orders. Project Title 2: Water payment system

Project Title 3: Meter Reading System

Developed a system that is run by utility companies that allows their employees to take meter readings digitally and the system automatically pushes the readings to a database that is read by a billing system and provides their clients with an accurate bill. It tackles the issue of Employees physically taking readings on paper and then re-entering the data onto the server that is read by the billing system.
